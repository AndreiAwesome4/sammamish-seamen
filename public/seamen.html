Our organization has been blessed by the Mormon Community for this amazing gym which serves as our practice and scrimmage location, as well as occasional breakfasts.

While the YMCA is avaliable for others, it is free of charge to the Seamen who will not only utilize its gym to get big and physical on the court but also the basketball courts which are full sized and give the real game experience.

Ebright Creek Park

The use of this outdoor park has recently been granted to the Seamen by the City of Sammamish. The wonderful outdoor courts will be utilized heavily in the summer and the warmer months when fresh air will be a priority for our players.
